Puerto Vallarta, Mexico

December 2017

Home › Mexico 2017 › Puerto Vallarta, Mexico

Post 1: Playa del Caballo

We finally made it to PV! Kudos to Alejandro who picked us up at 3am. Fabulous first day of adventure: a hike to Playa del Caballo along the coastline and lunch at the most fabulous Casitas Maraika. A hidden (-ish) gem.

Post 2: Las Mariettas

An early start to our Las Marietta’s snorkelling day, catching the sunrise on Banderas Bay. Beautiful fish and a bunch of humpbacks dropped in for a visit.

Post 3: Vallarta Adventures

An adventuresome day! Zip lining, waterfall rappelling, free jumping and more! Vallarta Adventures rocks.
