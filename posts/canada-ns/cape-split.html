Hiking Cape Split

Marking the end of the North Mountain Range, Cape Split runs between the Bay of Fundy and the Minas Basin. Apparently this headland has been separated from the mainland thanks to the force of the incoming and outgoing tides which are the highest in the world. 600 billion tonnes of water flowing twice a day! A stunning hike with a picnic at the tip. Thanks to Carolyn Mallory and Mark for an amazing day!
