Cape Breton Highlands National Park

What a fabulous road trip to Cape Breton with Evan. We originally thought we might bike the Cabot Trail…until a local’s advice caused us to reconsider. A good choice indeed! Narrow shoulders, big hills and curves galore. Kudos to those who ride it. There are two distinct camps of opinion as to taking a clockwise vs. counterclockwise route. We elected to go counterclockwise, starting in Baddeck, through Ingonish, Chéticamp and Inverness with hiking and lookoff stops along the way! A little brush with fame our first night; we stayed in the Telegraph Hotel, apparently in the same room Alexander Graham Bell and his wife Mabel stayed in. Perhaps that was while they were building their acreage, a short distance up the road, where he spent the last 35 years of his life.
