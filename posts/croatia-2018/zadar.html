Krka National Park

Loving the town of Zadar where we have landed for the night after a walk through Krka National Park. The park has a buildup of travertine (limestone deposits from the ground water) that has formed into pools and has built stunning waterfalls. An hour away is the small town of Zadar where the boardwalk has the most amazing sea symphony, pipes amplifying sounds from the sea that resemble whale song.

Plitvice Lakes National Park

Gorgeous day hiking at Plitvice Lakes National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Sixteen large and small lakes connected by tons of waterfalls.
