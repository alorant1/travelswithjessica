Tokyo, Japan

August 2017

Home › Japan 2017 › Tokyo, Japan

Post 1: Chuo

Our first whirlwind day in Tokyo started with a wake up at 1:45 am for the tuna auction at Tsukiji Fish Market, followed by our first ever sushi breakfast.

Post 2: Asakusa Shrine & Senso-ji Temple

Fabulous day of culture today with a visit to Asakusa Shrine and Senso-ji Temple. Turns out today was a festival so lots of people out in kimonos. We followed with some lounging for lunch and then a visit to the Edo museum where we had a great tour guide.

Post 3: Kanazawa, Ishikawa

So hot and humid in Kanazawa, a lovely medieval town a few hours outside Tokyo with a spectacular train station.
