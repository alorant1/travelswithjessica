Delicious Dordogne

Why Dordogne? Honestly, one of my favourite guys at a wine store in Vancouver told me about it years ago. He and his wife would rent a holiday home there for month every year. I wondered how they didn’t get bored going to the same region again and again. So when we were throwing a dart at the map to decide where to go after Bordeaux, Dordogne came to mind. Now I can see why he and his wife returned year after year. Truffles, foie gras, markets, châteaux and many of the plus beaux villages de France. It’s a place to relax, to explore, to simply enjoy. Bliss, really. And the locals are absolutely lovely.
