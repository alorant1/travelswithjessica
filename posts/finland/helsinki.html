Chilly day visiting the Rock Church, Sibelius Monument and the super cute Regatta Café.

Suomenlinna Island

A windy day circumnavigating Suomenlinna Island. Only accessible by water, the sea fortress dates back to 1748 when Finland was a part of Sweden. Its history started as a naval base for Sweden during the war between Sweden and Russia, then a naval base for Russia after the war between Russia and Finland, a POW camp during the Finnish Civil War, a submarine and anti-aircraft base during WWII, and finally a UNESCO World Heritage Site for its outstanding military architecture. Eight hundred people now live here.
