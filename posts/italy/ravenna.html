Ravishing Ravenna

This little gem has among the most remarkable Byzantine mosaics imaginable. Monreale in Sicily was incredible and Ravenna is equally amazing and dare I say maybe more so. Eight UNESCO world heritage sites in a pretty small town is quite an accomplishment and these places are unbelievably well-preserved.
