Non-stop Naples

Coming from small Italian towns and villages, Naples was a shock. At first glance, it comes across as gritty, dirty, crowded and noisy. But there’s a layer of unpretentious vitality that rises to the surface if you give it a chance. Naples is a young, frenetic city that is all about food, street music, graffiti and vehicles honking their way through pedestrian streets.
