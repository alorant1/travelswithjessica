Phallic Pompeii

How to describe Pompeii…enormous, unbelievable, best snapshot of 2000 years ago possible…well, all that and phallic. I had no idea the ancient Romans had such an obsession with sex and penises. While things were fairly equal and women were also encouraged to enjoy sexual freedoms, it’s still all about the penis in Pompeii.
