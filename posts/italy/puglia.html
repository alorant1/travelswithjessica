Picturesque Puglia

As we dance across Puglia between the Ionian and Adriatic seas, we are struck by the intense natural beauty and varied villages in the heel of the Italian boot. From tacky to terrific, some villages and landscapes are jaw-droppingly magical and others deserve varying degrees of a nod. We are thrilled to have our travel partners, Julie and Kevin!
