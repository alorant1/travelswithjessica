Harrison Lake

An overnight jaunt to Harrison Lake. Our first time and we unknowingly timed our visit with their annual music festival. Great hiking out here! Campbell Lake Trail was challenging and Sandy Cove/Whippoorwill Trail was much more relaxing. Glad we hiked in that order!
